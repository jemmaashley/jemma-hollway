Hi, I'm Jemma. I do most things you might expect and many things you won't. Come on, I'll show you.

This is a style guide for a website I wish to make. This is what I've been taught is example text. It's not much more interesting than lorem ipsum, but I thought it might look like I'd put more work if I tried to string together a few real words.

Actually, the more you look at words the more they look less like words. What do any of these letters even mean? The fact that Harper Lee's 'To Kill a Mockingbird' was written using the same 26 little symbols as this piece of shit is so proposterous that it's almost magical. I guess writing is a little bit like floristry... ironic really, considering floristy is exactly what my high-school English teacher threatened me with if I didn't learn to keep my head down and stop trying to be funny.

Sometimes when I finish writing something it feels like I've put together a puzzle, and no matter how much strife it took me to get there that feeling almost makes it all worth it in the end. If I practise enough I think one day I might learn how to arrange my letters in just the right way to change the world (or at least somebody's).
