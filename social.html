The social network.

Lady Bower Kitchen, Reservoir

October 2018 - January 2020

I worked as the Social Media Manager for Lady Bower Kitchen for just over a year. This role was lots of fun and allowed me to form a solid understanding of social media marketing, digital strategy and client communication. In this role I worked to take and edit photos for the page, schedule posts, respond to all DM's and messages over Instagram and Facebook, design and produce new menus, and design graphics for the page.
